About Us

New Remedy Pest Management (NRPM) is an integrated pest management industry leader offering safe and effective pest services in Kolkata. We protect homes and businesses from all types of pests. Any business or house located in or around these areas can avail themselves of our services. We offer professional and friendly pest management service to all our loyal clients. Our latest knowledge and skills provide our clients with the best solutions for their pest control needs. Established in 2007 as an ISO 9001:2015 Certified Organization, & with the help of Bayer, we successfully achieving target till the date by helping various sector people by it’s service. Through the guideline of Bayer, we make the Pest Management Service to you all as a Pest Free Zone through ecofriendly chemical.
